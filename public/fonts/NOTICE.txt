Maru Buri Light

This project uses a WOFF2 conversion of the official MaruBuri-Light.otf file.
NAVER provides Maru Buri for free web use under the license described on its
official Hangeul font page.

Official font page: https://hangeul.naver.com/font
Official download archive: https://hangeul.pstatic.net/hangeul_static/webfont/zips/maruburi.zip

The font software remains subject to NAVER's official font license terms.
